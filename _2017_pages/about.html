About

Functional Scala 2019 (London Edition) is an independent conference by the community and for the community, with no commercial interets, and focused only on Scala.

Functional Scala 2019 hopes to bring together some of the most passionate software developers working in the Scala programming language for two days of fun-filled talks, workshops, and panels.

Thanks to sponsors, the event is free of charge (although donations are encouraged for those able to afford).
